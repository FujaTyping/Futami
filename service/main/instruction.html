● Music command

Prefix Name Usage (Description)
/ lyrics Show lyrics of the song
f. filter Make your song more funny  ★
f. loop Loop song (usage : enter off , song or queue)
f. play Play song and join (usage : enter song name or url)
f. pause pause a song
f. queue See song queue
f. shuffle Shuffle song queue
f. skip/td> Skip current song
f. stop Stop current song and leave
f. volume Change volume (usage : usage : enter value of volume)
